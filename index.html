Our Story

More Than Clothes —
It's a Legacy

Above The Law was born from a simple belief: the best clothes have already been made. Founded in Accra with a passion for fashion that transcends trends, we source and curate pieces that have stood the test of time.

"Every garment tells a story. We find the ones worth wearing again."

Our pieces are hand-selected from across the globe and brought to you with care, authenticity, and the promise that what you wear matters — not just to you, but to the world.

Authenticity

Every piece is verified vintage, sourced with integrity.

Sustainability

Giving garments new life is our form of activism.

Quality First

Inspected, cleaned, and ready to wear — always.

Community

Built in Accra, worn around the world.
